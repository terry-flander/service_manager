Customer Feedback Email
When a booking job is marked Paid, ServiceDesk can offer to send the customer a thank-you / feedback email with a pre-filled link to your Google Form.
Manage templates under Email Templates. Use {{'{{'}}feedback_link{{'}}'}} in the body wherever you want the form link to appear.
Paste the "Get pre-filled link" URL from Google Forms, then replace the sample values you typed with {name} and {reference} in place of the matching entry.XXXXXXX= values.
