Our Story

About The Flying Bike

The Flying Bike is a mobile bicycle workshop that comes to your home or workplace to service and repair your bike. We service all kinds of bikes and fix all kinds of issues.

From city bikes to mountain bikes, daily commuters to occasional riders — we provide your bike with professional, loving care so you can ride like a boss! We treat every bike as if it were our own: with pride and with care, using quality parts and providing effective advice.
